# Photophobia Firefox Theme

A theme that let you adjust colors shades and bright for texts and panels dynamically and easily.

Every combination has a perfect look and feel.

### Why it is so great?
- Real‑time sliders and theme preview.
- Theme is fully generated, all possible variable is adjusted. 
  - The classic FirefoxColor leave a lot of areas uncustomized and is pretty annoying..
- Lightweight & private: tiny extension, local settings only — no telemetry.
- Crystal clear text: separate controls for text and panels keep content readable without losing style.
- Instant comfort: tweak contrast, brightness, hue and saturation with live preview — no restarts.
- Ready presets: switch between Night, Day, and High‑Contrast in one click.

Features at a glance
- Independent panel vs. text adjustments
- Save, apply, and reset presets
- Accessibility-minded defaults for light sensitivity and low vision

Quick tips
- Open the toolbar icon to access controls instantly.
- Try the Night preset to reduce glare in low light.
- Save a preset for your favorite reading setup and switch it anytime.

Privacy & accessibility
- Designed to improve legibility and reduce eye strain.
- All settings are stored locally in your browser — no data leaves your machine.

Compatibility
- Works with modern Firefox desktop releases.
